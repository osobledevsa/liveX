Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about LiveX and our influencer marketing platform.

Getting started as an influencer is easy! Simply sign up for an account, complete your profile with your social media accounts and niche information, and get verified by our team. Once verified, you can browse available campaigns, apply to those that match your audience, and start collaborating with brands.

At LiveX, we have a rigorous verification process for all influencers. We analyze their social media accounts, engagement rates, content quality, and audience demographics. Only influencers who meet our quality standards are approved on the platform, ensuring brands connect with authentic creators.

Influencers get paid every Saturday for completed campaigns. Once a campaign is finished and the content is approved by the brand, payment is processed automatically. We support various payment methods including mobile money, bank transfers, and PayPal, making it convenient for influencers across Africa.

We categorize influencers into four tiers based on their follower count: Nano (1K-10K), Micro (10K-100K), Macro (100K-500K), and Mega (500K+). Each tier has different pricing structures. For example, Nano tier starts at Ksh50,000 for 10 influencers for 1 week, while Micro tier starts at Ksh100,000. Mega tier pricing is customized based on specific requirements.

Brands have access to a comprehensive dashboard where they can track campaign performance in real-time. The dashboard provides detailed analytics including reach, engagement, clicks, conversions, and ROI. Brands can also see performance reports for each influencer and compare results across different campaigns.
