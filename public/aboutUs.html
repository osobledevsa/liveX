About LiveX Africa

Your Influencer Marketing Relationship Company

What is LiveX Africa?

Welcome to LiveX Africa, the pioneering influencer marketing platform dedicated to empowering the next generation of Somali and African creators globally. We are more than a platform; we are an economic accelerator connecting authentic, high-engagement voices to regional and international brands.

LiveX Africa strategically focuses on the intersection of the African diaspora and East African markets, with a strong presence connecting talent in key regions like Kenya, the broader Horn of Africa, and the global Somali diaspora.

Our Bold Mission: From Influence to Income

100,000

Jobs by 2028

We fundamentally believe that influence is a powerful force for opportunity, creativity, and economic stability, not merely a source of entertainment or a cultural concern. While some communities view social media content creation negatively, we see a massive, undervalued professional workforce.

Our mission is to unlock this potential, creating formal, sustainable careers. Our goal is to create 100,000 professional jobs for Somali and African creators by 2028.

The LiveX Advantage

The creator economy is exploding across Africa, but our focus on the Somali community fills a critical market gap. Somali creators offer brands unparalleled cultural authenticity and community trust, especially given the documented growth rate exceeding 1500% in this sector in recent years.

Cultural Authenticity

Somali creators bring genuine cultural perspectives that resonate deeply with audiences, creating authentic connections that traditional advertising cannot achieve.

Exponential Growth

With documented growth rates exceeding 1500%, the Somali creator economy represents one of the fastest-growing digital markets in Africa and globally.

Global Reach

Our creators span East Africa and the global diaspora, offering brands access to a borderless cultural and economic network with significant purchasing power.

How We Deliver

We make influencer marketing simple, transparent, and high-impact. Our end-to-end service manages the entire collaboration lifecycle, ensuring success for both creators and brands.

Data-Driven Matchmaking

We use proprietary AI and data analysis to match brands with verified creators whose values and audience demographics guarantee authentic resonance.

End-to-End Campaign Strategy

We manage everything from initial concept development and content briefing to execution and regulatory compliance.

Transparent Performance & ROI

Real-time analytics and detailed reporting measure engagement, reach, and tangible Return on Investment (ROI).

Fair & Formalized Contracts

Seamless Cross-Border Payments

Professional Creator Incubation
