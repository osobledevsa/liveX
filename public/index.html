How It Works

Our streamlined process makes influencer marketing simple and effective for both brands and creators.

Brand Creates Campaign

Brand sets up a campaign with requirements, budget, and goals. They pay for the campaign upfront.

Admin Approves Campaign

Our team reviews and approves the campaign to ensure it meets platform standards and guidelines.

Right Influencer Joins

Influencers who match the campaign criteria are invited to join and accept the campaign requirements.

Influencer Does the Work

Influencer creates and submits content according to the campaign requirements and deadlines.

Admin Reviews Work

Our team reviews the submitted content to ensure it meets campaign requirements and quality standards.

Work Approved & Payment

If the work meets requirements, it's approved and the influencer gets paid on Saturday after a week has passed.

Resubmit if Needed

If the work doesn't meet requirements, the influencer gets feedback and can resubmit for review.

The Ultimate LiveX Africa FAQ

Find answers to common questions about LiveX and our influencer marketing platform.

General

Livex Africa is an Influencer Marketing platform and Creator Agency, based in Nairobi, Kenya, dedicated to connecting Somali Creators (in Kenya, Somalia, East Africa, and the global diaspora) with brands, agencies, and commerce opportunities. We provide creators with brand partnerships, training, monetization tools, and payments support so their creativity becomes sustainable work.

Somali creators represent a culturally rich, globally connected, and highly entrepreneurial community. They speak to both local audiences in Kenya/Somalia/East Africa and a vast, engaged diaspora community across the globe. By building a specific ecosystem around this community, Livex Africa unlocks unique cultural authenticity, brand relevance, and powerful economic opportunity for creators and brands alike.

Livex Africa's mission is to generate 100,000 creator jobs by 2028 for Somali-affiliated talent worldwide. We believe in converting digital influence and creativity into real, sustainable income, providing clear pathways for Somali youth and diaspora creators to build lasting careers in the digital economy.

Our headquarters and operations team are based in Nairobi, Kenya, positioning us perfectly at the digital crossroads of East Africa and the Horn.

For Brands & Businesses

Brands can use our platform to discover vetted Somali Creators who speak Somali, English, Arabic (or relevant diaspora languages), deeply understand Somali culture, and have engaged audiences in Somalia, East Africa, or the global diaspora. We offer Campaign Management & Matching, Contract Support, Analytics, and streamlined payment flow.

The Somali market is a "leapfrog" digital economy. It has one of the highest mobile money penetration rates in the world (73%+), the cheapest mobile data in Africa, and 13.7 million internet users. It is a mobile-first, digital-native audience. However, trust is the most valuable commodity. Our creators are the "trust bridge" you need to authentically enter and succeed in this market.

We provide performance analytics dashboards showing reach, engagement rates, conversions (where applicable), audience demographics, and full campaign summaries. These KPIs help you see the value of working with Somali creators, align on objectives, and optimize future collaborations.

We support a wide range of collaboration formats, including sponsored posts (TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook), in-depth product reviews, affiliate commerce, brand ambassador programs, short-form video campaigns, and livestream events.

For Somali Creators

Any Somali-affiliated Creator who produces quality digital content can apply! This includes creators based in Nairobi, Mombasa, Hargeisa, London, Minneapolis, Toronto, or anywhere else in the world. We support everyone from beginners with a passionate, growing audience to full-time professional influencers.

  3. Training & Support: We provide access to mentorship, workshops, content-strategy sessions, and brand-safety tutorials to help you grow.
